Parking

We recommend that you park in the Green -1 or -2 car parks when you visit the cinema as they are the closest ones to the cinema. The car park exits remain open so that all of our patrons can still access their cars to leave the carpark late at night.

Also for patrons arriving after 6pm, you can pay your parking cost of €2, at the payment machines, as soon as you park to save you queing up after your movie.

There is also a seperate independent car park across the road from the cinema at the recycling centre that is free after 7pm (If you can get a space).
